# MTOS Product Document

This workspace uses the canonical product requirements in `docs/mtos-prd.md`.

## Scope

- MTOS is an AI-powered Account Management Operating System centered on Monthly Touch workflows.
- It is not a CRM, project management tool, or generic client portal.
- The product supports multi-tenant SaaS delivery for agencies managing large client portfolios.

## Core Capabilities

- Client Intelligence Engine
- AI Router
- Prompt Management Center
- Learning Engine
- Ownership-based visibility
- Monthly Touch lifecycle automation with approval gates
- QA, coaching, retention, roadmap, reviews, and operational accountability workflows
